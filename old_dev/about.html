About US

Who we are

We are a software development outsourcing consultancy with an R&D Center based in the heart of Kyiv, Ukraine.

Our ecosystem

By choosing to work with us, you get access to our unique technology ecosystem, which allows you to shorten time to hire software developers, time to market your software product, and focus entirely on your core competencies.

What we do

We provide scalable software solutions for companies around the world, from short-term projects to extended Agile teams, and deliver quality and ROI-driven solutions by working closely with our partners.

We don’t use any bench resources; as a client, you get an exclusive team’s setup based on your allotted budget and project specific requirements! All tech talents involved in your project development are committed to helping you reach your business goals and solve technology needs. This is our promise and guarantee!

Whether you're looking to build a software product from scratch, boost your in-house/onshore team’s capacity with auxiliary tech talent and external expertise, or outsource your entire R&D offshore – we have different value propositions to get you covered and help you successfully achieve your digital transformation and other business goals.
